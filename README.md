## 🛠 Features

CanvasX offers tools to visualize complex information. You navigate an infinite plane that grows as you draw. 

- **Hand-Drawn Style:** Shapes appear organic and casual, which helps focus on the flow of thoughts rather than perfect graphics.
- **Binding Arrows:** Connect objects with lines. When you move an object, the connecting lines adjust to stay attached.
- **Sticky Notes:** Place colored notes anywhere on the canvas to track tasks or reminders.
- **Tables:** Organize structured data within cells.
- **Code Blocks:** Paste your programming work into the canvas. The engine adds colors to your code to make it readable.
- **Canvas Search:** Use the search bar to find specific text or nodes within your large diagrams.
- **Live Collaboration:** Invite others to work on the same canvas. The encryption ensures only invited participants access your data.

## ⚙️ How to use the software

When you open CanvasX, you see a blank screen. Use your mouse or trackpad to pan around the surface.

**Drawing Shapes**
Select a tool from the top menu bar. Click and drag on the canvas to place a shape. You can resize objects by dragging the corners. 

**Working with Text**
Double-click any object to type inside it. The canvas automatically adjusts the container size as you add more text.

**Connecting Items** 
Select the arrow tool. Click one shape and drag the line to another shape. The software snaps the arrow to the edge of the object. 

**Saving your work**
CanvasX saves your progress automatically. You can also export your canvas as an image file or a portable data file for sharing with others. 

## 🔐 Privacy and Security

You own your data. End-to-end encryption scrambles your canvas information before it leaves your computer. This means that no outside party, including the software host, can view the contents of your drawings or notes. The privacy model ensures that your brainstorming sessions remain confidential.

## 🧩 Plugin Architecture

CanvasX supports various element types through plugins. If you find a gap in your workflow, you can add support for new diagram types. Developers build these modules to extend the base functionality. You manage these plugins through the settings menu.

## 💡 Tips for better diagrams

- Use keyboard shortcuts to switch tools quickly. You can find a complete list of these shortcuts in the Help menu.
- Group related objects together to move them as a single unit or to change their colors at the same time.
- Zoom in and out using your mouse wheel. This lets you see the details of a small section or the big picture of a large map.
- Label your arrows to clarify the meaning behind the connection between two notes.

Yes. You can work without an internet connection. Your changes sync to the cloud once you go back online.

The canvas is infinite. Hardware limits on your device determine how many elements you can add before the performance drops. 

**How do I update the application?**
CanvasX notifies you automatically when a new version is ready. Follow the prompts to download the latest installer and overwrite your existing version.
